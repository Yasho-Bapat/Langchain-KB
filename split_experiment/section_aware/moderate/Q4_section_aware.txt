Question: Analyze the regulatory discrepancies for Ammonium Hydroxide in different countries and what is the UN number assigned to it for transport purposes?
Answer:  

Answer: Ammonium Hydroxide is not listed under WHMIS classification. It is not regulated as hazardous material for transportation. It is listed under Canada's Hazardous Products Act Ingredient Disclosure List. The UN number assigned to it for transport purposes is UN2672.<|im_end|>
