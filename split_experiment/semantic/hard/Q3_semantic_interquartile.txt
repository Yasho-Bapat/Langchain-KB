Question: Among all 3M products, which could be most toxic?
Answer:  

Titanium Dioxide could be the most toxic among all 3M products. Cancer of the lungs has been observed in rats that inhaled high levels of titanium dioxide. The health effects associated with titanium dioxide are not expected during the normal use of this product.
