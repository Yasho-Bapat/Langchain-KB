Question: What is the proper shipping name of Ammonium Hydroxide and under which regulatory agencies is it listed?
Answer:   Answer: The proper shipping name of Ammonium Hydroxide is not regulated as hazardous material for transportation. It is not listed under Superfund Amendments and Reauthorization Act (SARA) 302 Extremely Hazardous Substances. It is listed under Superfund Amendments and Reauthorization Act (SARA) 311/312 Hazardous Chemicals. It is not regulated as hazardous material for transportation. It is present in Canada Domestic Substances List / Non-Domestic Substances List (DSL/NDSL). It is present in United States of America Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) List. It is not listed in European Inventory of Existing Commercial Chemical Substances (EINECS), European List of Notified Chemical Substances (ELINCS), and No Longer Polymers (NLP). It is not listed in California Proposition 65. It is not listed in Pennsylvania Right-to-Know Hazardous Substances. It is corrosive in New Jersey Worker and Community Right-to-Know Components.<|im_end|>
