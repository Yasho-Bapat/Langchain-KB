Question: What should be done to prevent from causing environmental contamination in the event of a spill?
Answer:  
Avoid runoff into storm sewers, ditches, and waterways. Contain spills with an inert absorbent material such as soil or sand. Prevent from spreading by covering, diking or other means. Provide ventilation. Clean up spills immediately observing precautions in the protective equipment section. Place into a suitable container for disposal. After removal, flush spill area with soap and water to remove trace residue.
