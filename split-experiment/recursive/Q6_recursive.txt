Question: What type of extinguishing media is suitable for a fire involving Citrisurf?
Answer:  The suitable extinguishing media for a fire involving Citrisurf is alcohol resistant foam, carbon dioxide, dry chemical, or water fog or spray. Unsuitable extinguishing media is not applicable. There are no specific hazards arising from the chemical and there are no unusual fire hazards. The special protective equipment and precautions for fire-fighters is to wear Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA), MSHA/NIOSH (approved or equivalent) and full protective gear. In any fire, the storage containers involved should be kept cool with water spray to prevent pressure build-up. Bursting of containers can occur at elevated temperatures. Empty drums should be completely drained, properly bunged and promptly returned to a drum reconditioner, or properly disposed of.
