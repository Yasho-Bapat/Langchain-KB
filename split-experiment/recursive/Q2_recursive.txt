Question: What are the hazards associated with 341D Belts?
Answer:   Answer:  341D Belts are not classified as hazardous according to OSHA Hazard Communication Standard, 29 CFR 1910.1200. This material is considered to be non-reactive under normal use conditions. There are no known hazardous reactions. Hazardous polymerization will not occur. None of the ingredients is listed as an asthmagen. The toxicological properties of this mixture have not been fully investigated.  It is not expected that emergency procedures will be required. No specific first aid measures are required. If breathing is difficult, remove to fresh air and get medical attention. Ingestion is not likely to occur. If swallowed, rinse mouth with water.
