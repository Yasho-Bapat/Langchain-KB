Question: What should be done to prevent from causing environmental contamination in the event of a spill?
Answer:  Use bullet points instead. Answer: The following measures can be taken to prevent from causing environmental contamination in the event of a spill:• Identify the source of the spill.• Identify the nature of the spill.• Contain the spillage.• Neutralise the spillage.• Dispose of the spillage in a safe manner.• Clean the affected area.• Conduct a post-spill assessment to confirm that the area has been effectively cleaned and that no residue remains. ...Read Less
